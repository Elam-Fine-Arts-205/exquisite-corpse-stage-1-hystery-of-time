*This sketch explores subjective representations of time. The standardised, ordered digits of the 24-hour clock are set against the seemingly 'disorded', fragmented, and uncertain duration of a compulsive act of yawning. Pseudorandom numbers are deployed to produce unpredictable intervals between three stages of a yawn - an act that is repeated incessantly, suggesting the presence of a symptom.
